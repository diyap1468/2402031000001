Our Story

Designed in
Silence.
Worn with Intent.

VELOUR was founded in 2018 by designer Kavya Arora in a small studio in Jaipur. What began as a quiet rebellion against fast fashion has grown into one of India's most respected luxury ready-to-wear labels.

Every VELOUR piece is designed to outlast a season — crafted from the finest natural fibers, assembled by artisans who have spent decades perfecting their craft.
